I need a website for movie and TV show lovers where people can discover, track, and discuss their favorite entertainment. 

Users should be able to easily find films, anime, and TV series by exploring popular top lists, checking upcoming release dates, or looking up specific actors, directors, and box office details. I want people to be able to find local theaters based on their location, check showtimes, and buy movie tickets while picking out their preferred seats. 

The site also needs to help users decide what to watch. They should be able to play trailers, read critic reviews, rate movies themselves, and see which streaming platforms are showing specific episodes. Tracking is very important, so users need to be able to build personal watchlists, mark titles as watched, schedule times for home viewing, and save things to a favorites collection. 

Finally, I want a strong community aspect. Fans should be able to join discussion groups for specific franchises, read and interact with community posts, share movie trivia, and even shop for physical media or downloadable videos.
