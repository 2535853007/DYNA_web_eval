I want to build a platform that helps people seamlessly find and apply for jobs. Users should be able to look for any kind of work—whether they want to be a local retail associate, a remote customer support agent, a high-earning software developer, or a delivery driver. They need a way to easily narrow down their options based on things that matter to them, like specific cities or zip codes, how far they are willing to commute, minimum salary expectations, education levels, and unique perks like medical insurance or veteran-friendly environments. 

Once they find the perfect role, the application process should be a breeze. Users should be able to apply directly using their saved details so they don't have to start from scratch every time, or easily get the links they need to fill out employer forms. Finally, everyone needs a personal space on the site to keep track of their career journey. They should be able to save interesting jobs to review later, see a clear history of what they’ve already applied for, and effortlessly check the current status of their past submissions to see if they are moving forward in the hiring process.
