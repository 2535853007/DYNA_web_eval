I want to build a comprehensive entertainment platform where people can discover, compare, and purchase a wide variety of media. Users should be able to easily explore books, movies, video games, board games, musical instruments, and even event tickets all in one place. 

The main goal is to let users find exactly what they are looking for, whether that means browsing by specific authors, languages, age groups, release dates, or formats like paperback versus digital. They should be able to compare prices to find the best deals, whether they want a cheap used gaming console, a newly released bestseller, or VIP concert tickets. 

When users are ready to check out, the process should be smooth. For physical goods, they should be able to arrange delivery to their address or opt for local pickup. For digital purchases, users need a personal digital library where they can instantly access, download, or stream their movies and games. 

Finally, users need a personal profile to manage their overall experience. They should be able to save favorite items to a wishlist, keep track of game pre-orders, review past receipts, and easily cancel an order if they change their mind.
