I want to build a platform that helps everyday people easily navigate legal, compliance, and government documentation. Users should be able to come to the site to find out how to handle common official tasks, like requesting a police crash report, transferring a vehicle title, or understanding landlord-tenant rules in their state. They should also be able to look up specific requirements for things like getting an adult passport, applying for a work visa, or knowing what documents they need to sell a car.

Beyond just reading information, users need to be able to take action. They should be able to register to vote, find their local polling locations, and set election reminders. The site also needs to let people search for public records like marriage registries or past court decisions, and request certified copies directly. 

Finally, users should have their own personal profile. This space will let them bookmark important local court contacts, save their application receipts for things like birth certificates, and build a personal library of important legal, civil, and tax forms that they can fill out and safely archive for the future.
