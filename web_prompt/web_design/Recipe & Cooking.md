I need a website for a cooking and recipe community where people can discover, save, and share their favorite meals. Users should be able to easily search for recipes based on very specific ingredients and dietary needs—like finding a highly-rated pumpkin dessert, an air fryer dish, or chicken recipes that specifically exclude rice. 

When someone finds a recipe they love, they should be able to save it to a personal favorites collection, print it out in a clean, kitchen-friendly format, or add the needed ingredients straight into a shopping cart. 

I also want this platform to have a strong community feel. Users need the ability to follow their favorite recipe authors, leave comments or ask questions on specific dishes, and publish their own helpful cooking tips for others to read. People should be able to easily share recipes with friends and leave heart reactions on photos of dishes that other users have successfully cooked. The overall goal is to create an inspiring, easy-to-use space for everyday home cooks to connect, find inspiration, and organize their kitchen adventures.
