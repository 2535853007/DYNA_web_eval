I want to build a content discovery and sharing platform where people can explore, save, and talk about their favorite topics. Users should be able to easily look up specific interests—like gaming updates, healthy recipes, or popular photos—and see what’s currently trending or getting the most engagement. The site also needs to be highly social. Users must be able to upload their own content, repost things they like to their own feeds, and share links directly with their general followers or curated groups of friends.
