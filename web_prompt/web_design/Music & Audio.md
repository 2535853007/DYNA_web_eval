I need an all-in-one music and audio platform for listeners, creators, and musicians. For everyday users, the site should let them stream songs, discover new artists, listen to podcasts, and read album reviews. They should be able to build personal collections, like saving favorite podcast episodes, making discovery playlists, or adding vinyl records to a wantlist. I also want users to easily find and buy concert tickets for upcoming tours around the world and get notified about them.

For video creators and commercial users, the platform needs a vast library of background audio. They should be able to find very specific tracks—like a happy 20-second tune, a tense track they can loop for a video, or commercial-use electronic music—and organize them for their projects. 

Finally, the site needs to cater to people learning music. It should allow them to search for guitar tabs based on difficulty, find song chords, and save them to a personal practice library. Users should have full control over their accounts to highlight their current track obsessions, manage bookmarks, and clear out their listening history completely whenever they want.
